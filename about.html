About Real Ghost Capture

Dedicated to documenting and preserving authentic paranormal evidence since 2010

Our Mission

At Real Ghost Capture, we are committed to the scientific investigation and documentation of paranormal phenomena. Our mission is to provide authentic, unaltered evidence of the supernatural while maintaining the highest standards of integrity in our research and investigations.

We believe that by sharing our findings with the world, we can contribute to a better understanding of the unexplained and bridge the gap between skepticism and belief through compelling, verifiable evidence.

Scientific Approach

We employ rigorous scientific methods and cutting-edge technology in all our investigations.

Authenticity

We never use special effects, staging, or manipulation in our evidence collection.

Education

We aim to educate the public about paranormal phenomena through factual documentation.

Our Story

Real Ghost Capture was founded in 2010 by Dr. James Wilson, a former skeptic and professor of psychology who had a life-changing encounter with the unexplained during a research trip to an abandoned asylum in New England.

What began as a personal quest for answers evolved into a dedicated team of researchers, investigators, and technical specialists who have conducted over 500 investigations across six continents.

Over the years, we've amassed one of the world's largest collections of authenticated paranormal evidence, including full-bodied apparitions, EVP recordings, poltergeist activity, and unexplained phenomena that challenge conventional understanding.

In 2015, we launched our subscription platform to share our findings with the world while funding increasingly sophisticated research and investigation methods.

Our Methodology

We employ a rigorous scientific approach to paranormal investigation, combining traditional methods with cutting-edge technology to document unexplained phenomena.

01

Research & Preparation

Before any investigation, we conduct thorough historical research, interview witnesses, and develop a site-specific investigation plan.

02

Controlled Environment

We establish baseline readings, document environmental conditions, and implement controls to eliminate contamination of evidence.

03

Multi-Method Documentation

We use multiple recording devices simultaneously, including full-spectrum cameras, thermal imaging, and specialized audio equipment.

04

Analysis & Verification

All evidence undergoes rigorous analysis by our technical team, including audio enhancement, video frame analysis, and debunking attempts.

05

Peer Review

Evidence that passes initial analysis is reviewed by a panel of experts, including skeptics, to ensure authenticity and eliminate alternative explanations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our work and paranormal investigations

Do you use special effects or stage your evidence?

Absolutely not. We have a strict policy against using any form of special effects, staging, or manipulation in our evidence collection. All footage in our archives is authentic and undergoes rigorous verification processes. We maintain complete transparency about our investigation methods and equipment used.

How do you verify that evidence is authentic?

We employ a multi-stage verification process that includes technical analysis of the raw footage, environmental factor assessment, multiple witness corroboration when available, and review by our scientific panel. We also attempt to debunk our own evidence by recreating conditions and testing alternative explanations before confirming authenticity.

Can I suggest a location for investigation?

Yes! We welcome location suggestions from our members. Premium and Ultimate subscribers can submit investigation requests through their member dashboard. Our team reviews all submissions and selects locations based on reported activity, historical significance, and logistical feasibility. If your location is selected, you'll receive credit and special access to the investigation results.

What equipment do you use in your investigations?

We use a comprehensive array of equipment including full-spectrum cameras, FLIR thermal imaging devices, EMF meters, digital audio recorders with specialized microphones, REM pods, SLS mapping cameras, and proprietary devices developed by our technical team. All equipment is regularly calibrated and tested to ensure accuracy.

Do you offer refunds if I'm not satisfied with the content?

Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new subscribers. If you're not satisfied with our content within the first two weeks, you can request a full refund. After this period, we handle refund requests on a case-by-case basis according to our refund policy, which you can find in our Terms of Service.

Can I join one of your investigations?

Ultimate tier subscribers have exclusive opportunities to join select investigations through our Member Investigation Program. These events are announced quarterly and have limited availability. Participants must be 18+ and sign liability waivers. We also host annual Paranormal Conferences where members of all tiers can meet our team and participate in workshops.
