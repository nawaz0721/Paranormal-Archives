Hospital Apparition

Apparition Full Body Hospital Night Vision

This chilling footage was captured during our overnight investigation at St. Mary's Hospital, an abandoned medical facility that closed its doors in 1985 after a series of unexplained deaths. The building has been vacant for decades, with numerous reports of paranormal activity from urban explorers and security personnel.

At 2:14 in the video, a full-bodied apparition of what appears to be a nurse in period clothing can be seen walking down the corridor. The figure is semi-transparent and moves with an unnatural gait before disappearing through a wall. No one else was present in the building during our investigation, and all team members were accounted for in other areas of the hospital.

Historical records show that a nurse named Elizabeth Blackwood died in this exact corridor in 1952 after contracting a mysterious illness from a patient. Witnesses have reported seeing a female figure in a nurse's uniform in this area for decades.

Equipment Used:

  • Full-spectrum night vision camera
  • Thermal imaging device
  • EMF meters (readings spiked to 7.2 mG during the apparition)
  • Digital audio recorders (EVPs captured and analyzed separately)

Investigation Notes:

Temperature dropped from 68°F to 52°F during the apparition sighting. Multiple team members reported feeling watched and experiencing a sense of dread in this area. Follow-up investigations have been scheduled to attempt further contact with the entity.
