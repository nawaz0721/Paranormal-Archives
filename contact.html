Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly will I receive a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. Premium and Ultimate subscribers receive priority support with responses typically within 12 hours.

How can I submit my own paranormal evidence for review?

You can submit evidence using our contact form above by selecting "Submit Evidence" as the subject. Please include as much detail as possible about when and where the evidence was captured, equipment used, and any relevant background information. Our team will review your submission and may contact you for additional information.

Can I request an investigation at my property?

Yes, you can request an investigation by selecting "Investigation Request" as the subject in our contact form. Due to high demand, we can only accept a limited number of investigation requests. Our team evaluates each request based on reported activity, historical significance, and logistical feasibility. If selected, we'll contact you to schedule a preliminary assessment.

How do I update my billing information or cancel my subscription?

You can update your billing information or cancel your subscription by logging into your account dashboard. Navigate to "Subscription Settings" where you'll find options to update payment methods or cancel your subscription. If you need assistance, contact our support team by selecting "Subscription Question" in the contact form above.
