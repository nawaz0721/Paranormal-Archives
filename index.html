Basement EVP

Recorded: March 15, 2025 Location: Blackwood Manor Duration: 2:18

This chilling EVP (Electronic Voice Phenomenon) was captured in the basement of Blackwood Manor, a 19th-century house with a dark history. Our team conducted an EVP session after reports of strange voices and unexplained sounds in the basement area.

At 0:42 in the video, a clear voice can be heard saying "help me" in response to our investigator's question. No one else was in the building at the time of the recording.
